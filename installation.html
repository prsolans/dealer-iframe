Installation Tips

  • A. Kick Out Flashing
    1 Water-resistive barrier laps over the step flashing and roofing felt
    2 Step flashing shall be a minimum 4 in. x 4 in. with a 100˚-110˚bend/kickout
  • B. Drip Edge
    1 Drip edge
    2 Sub-fascia (optional)
  • C. Valley Shingle Extensions
    1 Extend shingles at least 1 in. out from the fascia when gutters are present
  • D. Gutter Clearance
    1 Gutter and end caps should end a minimum 1 in. from siding
  • E. Roof to Wall
    1 (HZ5) Minimum 2 in. clearance and (HZ10) Minimum 1 in. clearance
  • F. Blocked Penetrations
    1 1/4 in. gap. Do not caulk. Z-Flashing
    2 Minimum 22.5˚ weather cut
  • G. Deck to Wall
    1 Minimum 2 in. clearance
    2 Z-Flashing
  • H. Horizontal Flashing
    1 1/4 in. gap. Do not caulk. 2 Flashing 3 Minimum 1/8 in. caulked gap
  • I. Ground to siding
    1 Minimum 6 in. clearance 2 Minimum 1/4 in. clearance
  • J. Sheltered Areas
    1 Flashing over trim with 1/4 in. gap
    2 Minimum 1/4 in. clearance
    3 Flashing
  • K. Mortar - Masonry
    1 1 L-Flashing
    2 1/4 in. gap. Do not caulk.
    3 Flashing
  • L. Slabs: Steps-Path-to Siding
    1 (HZ5) Minimum 2 in. clearance from slab and (HZ10) minimum 1 in. clearance from slab
